Scoring

Evaluation Criteria

Your chatbot is judged across 7 parameters totaling 100 points.

20%
Problem Understanding
  • Clarity in understanding the domain
  • Logical structuring of the solution
  • Practical usefulness of the chatbot
10%
Model Selection
  • Choice of LLM
  • Suitability for the task
  • Reasoning behind selection
20%
Prompt Engineering
  • Clarity and structure of prompts
  • Context handling
  • Instruction design
20%
Accuracy & Relevance

10%
Latency & Performance
